IT114116 / ITP4827 Web Analytics 

Hong Kong Institute of Information Technology

Project Based Learning 



Module Intended Learning Outcome:

On completion of the module, students are expected to be able to:



Understand the web analytics industry and career


Develop Implementation plan for the planning of web optimization for website


Implement web site tracking by using Web Analytics Tools

Lesson Intended Learning Outcome:

On completion of this project, students are expected to be able to:



Create Web Analytics Measurement Plan


Report and Implement Web Analytics skills on website’s performance



Digital Growth: Using GA4 to Improve <Your website name> Performance


Phase 1: GA4 Implementation and Tracking

Objective: Prove you can correctly "tag" a site and capture data.

Action: Create your website (using Google Sites, Wix, GithubPage…etc).



Setup: Create a GA4 property and install the tracking code.



Custom Tracking: Use the GA4 Events Interface to create one GA4 Custom Event (e.g., clicking a "Contact Me" button or a "Download CV" link).



Deliverable: A public live URL and a screenshot of the Realtime Report showing your own activity in the GA4 dashboard.


Phase 2: Business Objective and KPIs Definition

Objective: Define a clear business objective for a hypothetical or existing web/app property. 


Here are some examples as reference:

1 Assume you are the boss of an e-commerce store. "Optimize the e-commerce checkout funnel." 

2 Assume you are a youtuber. "Analyze content engagement for your travelling vlog."

3 Assume your team is promoting your FYP. "Boost more web site landing page traffic to attract more potential employers to contact your teammates for further business opportunities."

4 Assume you are a startup founder. "Sign up more newsletter and grow your business."



Deliverable: A 1-page "Measurement Plan" listing your web/app property, the defined Business Objective and about 2-3 specific measurable KPIs.


Phase 3: Insights, Analysis and Exploration

Objective: Use GA4’s exploration tool to find and fix a problem of <Your website name>.


Action:  Analyse your user behavior using one GA4’s exploration tool in order to meet your website’s Business Objective 

Task:   

Here are some examples as reference:

1 Use GA4 Funnel Exploration to identify the specific step where most customers drop off during your checkout process in your e-commerce store.

2 Use GA4 Path Exploration to discover the unpredictable routes users take after landing on your homepage.

3 Use GA4 User Exploration to examine the individual activities and click-streams of your highest-spending customers.

4 Use GA4 Segment overlap Exploration to identify how different customer segments such as mobile users and returned visitors intersect 


Deliverable: Both video and screenshots of a GA4 exploration tool used and a report on how to fix specific problems. (e.g., Targeting a 10% increase in the Custom Event “Download CV”)


Evaluation Criteria

Projects will be assessed on:

Correctness and completeness of GA4 tracking and configuration.


Clarity of the defined business objective and alignment of KPIs to the objective.


Technical Accuracy: Do the tags fire? Is the Custom Event showing up?



Strategic Thinking: Do the KPIs actually match the Business objective?



Analytical Insight: Can a "problem" be identified in the Exploration tool?
